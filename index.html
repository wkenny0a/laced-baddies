Individual strands are plucked out to create a soft, natural gradient density—thin at the very edge and gradually getting thicker. You can perform a simple "burn test" on a few strands: real human hair will burn quickly, produce white smoke, and turn entirely into fine ash when crushed. Synthetic hair, by contrast, will melt, produce harsh black smoke, and leave a hard, sticky plastic ball that cannot be crushed.

You might experience a few loose static flyaway hairs initially out of the package, which resolves completely after the first wash. Like all long hair, we recommend gently brushing from the tips upwards to prevent friction tangling.

Wearing our nude nylon bald cap (which comes FREE in your Welcome Kit) holds your natural braids or wraps flat and creates a uniform, skin-toned foundation. This hides the dark contrast of your natural hair underneath, making the micro-bleached knots of our Ultra-HD Lace™ look completely invisible against your skin.

We recommend taking it off every night before bed. Since our wigs are 100% glueless and secured by our comfortable, adjustable CrownGrip™ velvet band, it takes only 2 seconds to slide it off. Taking it off nightly lets your scalp breathe, prevents sweat and moisture buildup (avoiding itchy scalps and odors), and makes it easy to massage your natural hair before sliding it back on in the morning.

When you take your wig off at night, place it on a wig stand, mannequin head, or safely inside our pink storage box. Keeping the wig resting flat on a stand prevents friction tangling, maintains the natural curve of the cap, and preserves the bouncy body wave pattern without pulling or stretching the strands.

Wash gently in cool or lukewarm water with sulfate-free shampoo. After washing, pat it dry gently with a microfiber towel (never wring or rub it aggressively). Place it on a wig stand to air-dry. Always avoid brushing or styling your wig while it is wet, as wet hair strands are more fragile and susceptible to stretching or shedding.

To keep the waves shiny and soft, we recommend a deep-conditioning treatment every couple of weeks. Avoid deep-conditioning daily to prevent product buildup. You only need to wash your wig after about 25 wears. Washing too frequently (daily or weekly) can dry out the natural oils of the human hair, reducing the life of your crown.
